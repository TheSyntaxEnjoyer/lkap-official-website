ABOUT US

Lionheart Karate Association Philippines Inc., known as Lionheart Karatedo Club, is a leading martial arts federation in the Philippines. Founded in 1996 by Shihan Rodolfo Salinas and led by Chairman Sensei Alrey Joseph Mesias, the club is dedicated to promoting and teaching traditional Japanese Karate. With over two decades of experience, Lionheart Karatedo Club offers a refined system of instruction for trainees of all ages, enhancing physical fitness, self-defense skills, discipline, and character development.

We uphold the values of respect, integrity, and perseverance, providing a supportive environment where students can thrive. Our comprehensive training programs, guided by experienced instructors, empower individuals to master the art of Karate and embody its virtues.

CREDENTIALS

Lionheart Karate Association Philippines, Inc. is proud to be a SEC-registered organization, ensuring our legitimacy and commitment to upholding the highest standards of karate instruction and organizational integrity. Our SEC registration reflects our dedication to transparency, accountability, and professional governance, providing peace of mind to our members and their families.

We are honored to serve as the official Philippine representative of the Asai Shotokan Association International (ASAI), a prestigious and traditional Japanese Shotokan Karate organization. As the ASAI representative in the Philippines, we are committed to preserving and promoting the rich heritage and disciplined practice of Shotokan Karate, adhering to the rigorous standards and principles established by ASAI.
